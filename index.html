Import from Google Sheets
Paste CSV exported from your Expense Tracker Google Sheet
Export Instructions
1. In your Google Sheet, go to File → Download → CSV (.csv)
2. Open the downloaded file in a text editor and copy all content
3. Paste below. Your sheet columns (any order):
DATE · ACCOUNT · CATEGORY · ITEM · AMOUNT · TO/FROM · CLOSING BALANCE (optional)

Date formats accepted: DD/MM/YYYY, DD/MM/YYYY HH:MM:SS, YYYY-MM-DD
Amount: negative = expense (e.g. -399.42), positive = income
Note: ₹ symbol, commas in numbers, and closing balance column are handled automatically.
After import: All unique accounts, categories, items & people are auto-created in Manage.
